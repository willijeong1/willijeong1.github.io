Welcome! My name is Yeonwoo Jeong, and I also go by "William."

I have always been curious about the mind, having encountered formative experiences and amazing people that shaped who I am. Envisioning the future of biological psychiatry with a warm heart, my research interests span psychoneuroimmunology, systems neuroscience, and resilience in human development. My two years dedicated to research and exploration have only deepened my resolve to dive into the harmonies within us, seeking the answers that matter most for mental health.

In my free time, I enjoy music and the performing arts, rooted in twelve years of classical piano. I was involved in student-led musical theatre productions some years ago, and I am currently excited about expressing everyday inspirations through a cappella. You might encounter some of my performances, among other things, in the [blog](/blog) section. Apart from that, I love scenic drives, travelling, and sharing unique cultures.
